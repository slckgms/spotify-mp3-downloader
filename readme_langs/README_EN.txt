ABOUT
-----
Spotify MP3 Downloader lets you download music for free in high quality
using Spotify playlist, album or track links.
Downloads are performed via YouTube Music.

FEATURES
--------
✓ Playlist, album and single track support
✓ MP3, M4A, OGG, OPUS, AAC, FLAC, WAV, AIFF, ALAC format support
✓ 128 / 192 / 256 / 320 kbps quality options
✓ Automatic metadata (title, artist, album)
✓ Cover art embedding (MP3 and M4A)
✓ Auto-retry on internet disconnect
✓ Playlists with 100+ tracks supported
✓ 12 language support

HOW TO USE
----------
1. Open the desired playlist/album/track on Spotify
2. Share > Copy Link to copy the link
3. Paste the link in the app (Ctrl+V or right-click > Paste)
4. Click "Fetch" — the track list will load
5. Choose format and quality
6. Click "Download"
7. When done, click "Open Folder" to access your music

SUPPORTED SPOTIFY LINK TYPES
-----------------------------
• https://open.spotify.com/playlist/...
• https://open.spotify.com/album/...
• https://open.spotify.com/track/...

NOTES
-----
• Internet connection required
• First download may take a moment (YouTube search is performed)
• For playlists with 100+ tracks, there may be a brief wait
  while fetching track details

How it works

Simple as 1, 2, 3

1

Copy Spotify Link

Open any playlist, album or track on Spotify. Share → Copy Link.

2

Paste & Fetch

Paste the link into the app. Click "Fetch" — track list loads instantly.

3

Choose & Download

Select your format and quality. Hit Download. Done.

✓

Enjoy Your Music

Files saved with full metadata and cover art. Ready to play anywhere.
